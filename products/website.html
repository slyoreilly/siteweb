What's about our web site

  • SyncStats offers a rich and complete web site experience that covers most of hockey related activities. Our purpose is that you can easily offer an astounding experience to hockey players of your league.

Features

  • Personnal stats: goals, assists, plus/minus, win/loss, goal against average...
  • Team Stats: Win, loss, standings, Power plays / Penalty kills...
  • League and game management tools: You make your rosters and it's all loaded to your phones
  • Goals Highlights, Photo galery
  • League, Team, Player, User profiles
  • Messaging system like post wall and user mail
  • Under some conditions, it can be nested in your existing site!
  • Still more. Actually, it's constantly increasing. You want something added? Tell us, we'll get it done!
