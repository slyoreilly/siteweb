SYNCBOARD

What's SyncBoard

  • Is a scoreboard that can be remotely controlledwith our SyncStats app. Can be displayed on any flatscreen and doesn't need an internet connection.

  • Different size avalaible: Small portable, Medium semi-portable (like for tournaments), Large (fixed)
  • No internet connection needed

Cost
